CREATING A NEW LESSON TAB
=================================================

1. Right-click on "Lesson 1" tab at bottom
2. Click "Duplicate"
3. Rename the new tab (e.g., "Animals", "Lesson 2", etc.)
4. Replace the data with new nouns
5. Update Row 1 & 2 with new title/subtitle
6. Access via: ?lesson=YOUR_TAB_NAME

Example:
If tab is named "Animals", URL becomes:
https://YOUR-DOMAIN/icelandic-noun-game-dynamic.html?lesson=Animals
